Born and raised by the Himalayas I have always felt at home in the sky looking at the earth from up above. My fascination with nature and our place in the universe inspired me to pursue a career in Urban Studies. I graduated from Lawrence University with a Bachelors degree in Geoscience and Economics and am currently pursuing a masters degree in Geospatial Technologies. I enjoy the little nuances of being human like meeting and networking with new people, spending time with my cat and three dogs, exploring nature and appreciating the beauty of being alive. I love to fly my drone around nature as it's the closest I can get to flying and being free.
